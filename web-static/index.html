WHAT WE DO

Every year there are about 20 lakh kids loosing their lives for missing vaccinations in India*. Various studies show that large number of kids miss their vaccination because of parents forgetting the vaccination scheduled dates. vRemind.org is a social enterprise committed for noble cause to improve immunization for kids. We help parents with timely reminders about vaccination schedule thus help in protecting their kids from various life threatening diseases.

Our Focus is to
Use simple yet effective approaches to reach everyone targeted for immunization
Ensure maximum reach across the country by increasing awareness for immunization
Provide free vaccination reminders upto 5 years of age
Remind about National Polio Vaccination by Govt. of India
